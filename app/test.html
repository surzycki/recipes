Achiote Chicken

mexican

Ingredients

1 kg chicken legs
100 g orange
70 g lemon
1 lime
2 tbsp white vinegar
2 tsp salt
2 tsp mexican oregano
1 tsp pepper
4 garlic cloves
1 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp achiote paste
Achiote chicken, from Mexico's Yucatán Peninsula, is marinated in a vibrant "recado rojo" mix of annatto seeds, spices, and citrus. Grilled or baked, it's a flavorful Central American staple often paired with rice or tortillas.

Marinate chicken legs overnight in juice from orange, juice from lemon, juice from lime, white vinegar, salt, mexican oregano, pepper, smashed garlic cloves, olive oil, and achiote paste

[chicken legs: 1 kg; orange: 100 g; lemon: 70 g; lime: 1 ; white vinegar: 2 tbsp; salt: 2 tsp; mexican oregano: 2 tsp; pepper: 1 tsp; garlic cloves: 4 ; olive oil: 1 tbsp; achiote paste: 2 tbsp]

Sous vide the chicken for 4 hours at 74°C, then finish in broiler.

Let rest 10 minutes before serving
